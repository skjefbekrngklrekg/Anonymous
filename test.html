ControlFlow: Streaming Action Prediction via Dynamic Magnitude Scaling

This page provides supplementary material for anonymous review. We present qualitative comparisons between Streaming Flow Policy and the proposed ControlFlow framework for streaming action prediction. All demonstrations are initialized from the same starting state.

Streaming Flow Policy (Baseline)

Behavior of Streaming Flow Policy under a fixed action magnitude. Starting from the same initial state, the baseline progresses conservatively and may require substantially more steps to approach the target. In some cases, the task may not be completed within the rollout horizon.

ControlFlow (Proposed)

ControlFlow with dynamic magnitude scaling. From the same initial state, the proposed method adapts the action magnitude during streaming inference, enabling faster task completion with fewer steps and improved robustness across demonstrations.
